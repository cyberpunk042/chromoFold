Local evidence workbench

Turn two result files into an auditable comparison.

Load a baseline and candidate result. The browser validates fingerprints, computes deltas, exposes regressions and produces a portable report. Files stay in this browser session and are never uploaded.

A browser PASS is a portable comparison result. It is not maintainer qualification and does not upgrade evidence maturity.

Load two `chromofold.evidence-result.v1` files to begin.

Need repeatability?

Promote the comparison into a session.

One matched pair is useful for exploration. Qualification Sessions aggregate at least three runs per side, calculate variability and export a support package.
