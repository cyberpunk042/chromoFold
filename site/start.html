Decision rule

Success means a workload improvement under matched conditions.

Capacity

More context or more concurrent requests under the same VRAM budget.

Performance

Acceptable TTFT and generation throughput for the selected goal.

Correctness

Bit-exact or contract-defined correctness before speed is reported.

Operations

Cancellation, failure, upgrade and rollback scenarios produce complete evidence.
