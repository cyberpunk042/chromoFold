Compatibility

Know what is supported before you install.

Compatibility is a versioned contract. Planned, experimental, candidate, qualified, and independently reproduced are different states—not marketing synonyms.

Status meanings

planned

No integration yet

Roadmap intent only. Do not claim support.

experimental

Engineering preview

Some components work, but the product path is incomplete.

candidate

Ready for qualification

The integration exists and requires exact-hardware evidence.

qualified

Passed defined gates

PASS evidence exists for a specific immutable target.

No silent fallback

Unsupported must be visible.

Generated profiles configure unsupported paths to fail rather than quietly switching to dense behavior and producing misleading results.

fallback.silent = false

Compatibility failures should be explicit and diagnosable.
